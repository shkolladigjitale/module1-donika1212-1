What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) mimics human intelligence to perform tasks like learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.

What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT, a model by OpenAI, helps users interact naturally with AI to enhance learning and assist with various tasks.

About ChatGPT

ChatGPT is an advanced conversational AI model created by OpenAI. It is designed to engage in human-like text-based conversations, offering answers, explanations, and personalized suggestions. Leveraging natural language understanding and generation, ChatGPT supports diverse applications, including education, problem-solving, and virtual assistance. Its flexibility and adaptability make it a powerful tool for enhancing digital interactions.
